# Investment Scams

## Overview

Investment scams often promise unusually high or guaranteed returns with little or no risk. Fraudsters may pressure people to act quickly or keep the opportunity secret.

## Warning Signs

- Guaranteed profits
- Pressure to invest immediately
- Requests for cryptocurrency or gift cards
- Unregistered investment companies
- Difficulty withdrawing money

## How to Protect Yourself

- Verify the company independently.
- Research the investment thoroughly.
- Be cautious of unsolicited offers.
- Never invest money you cannot afford to lose.

## If You Have Been Targeted

- Stop sending money.
- Contact your bank or payment provider promptly.
- Preserve messages, receipts, and other evidence.
- Report the incident to the appropriate authorities in your country.
