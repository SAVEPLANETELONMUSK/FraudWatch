# Romance Scams

## Overview

Romance scammers build trust over time before asking for money, gifts, or financial assistance.

## Warning Signs

- Rapid declarations of love.
- Excuses for never meeting in person.
- Requests for money.
- Stories involving emergencies.
- Pressure to keep the relationship secret.

## Staying Safe

- Verify identities independently.
- Be cautious about sending money.
- Talk with trusted friends or family if you're unsure.

## If You Have Been Targeted

- Stop communication if you believe it is a scam.
- Preserve evidence.
- Report the incident through appropriate channels.
- Seek emotional support if needed.
