# Scam Alert 002

## Title

Romance Scam Warning

## Priority

High

## Summary

Scammers build trust before requesting money or financial assistance.

## Warning Signs

- Refuses video calls or in-person meetings
- Asks for money unexpectedly
- Claims emergencies requiring urgent funds
- Encourages secrecy

## Recommended Action

Stop sending money, preserve evidence, and seek advice from trusted people if you have concerns.
