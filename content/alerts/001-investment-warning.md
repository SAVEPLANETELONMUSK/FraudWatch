# Scam Alert 001

## Title

High-Risk Fake Investment Platforms

## Priority

High

## Summary

Fraudsters may advertise guaranteed returns and pressure victims to invest quickly.

## Warning Signs

- Guaranteed profits
- Pressure to act immediately
- Requests for cryptocurrency
- Unlicensed investment companies
- Difficulty withdrawing funds

## Recommended Action

Do not send money until you have independently verified the investment and the company.
