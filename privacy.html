Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This page explains how FraudWatch handles information shared through the website.

Information You Share

When you contact FraudWatch or submit a report, you may choose to provide information such as your name, email address, contact details, or a description of the suspected scam.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to respond to enquiries, improve educational resources, understand fraud trends, and enhance the FraudWatch platform.

Your Privacy

FraudWatch does not ask for passwords, one-time verification codes (OTPs), banking PINs, recovery phrases, or other highly sensitive credentials. Please never send this information through the website, email, Telegram, or WhatsApp.

Third-Party Services

FraudWatch may use trusted third-party services for website hosting, communications, analytics, or future platform improvements. These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You may contact FraudWatch if you have questions about information you have shared or wish to request its removal, where appropriate.
