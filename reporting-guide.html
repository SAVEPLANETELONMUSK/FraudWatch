Evidence & Reporting Guide

Collecting accurate information can help you make a clear report to the appropriate organisation or authority.

What to Collect

  • 📱 Phone numbers
  • 📧 Email addresses
  • 🌐 Website addresses (URLs)
  • 💬 Chat usernames (Telegram, WhatsApp, social media)
  • 📷 Screenshots of conversations
  • 💳 Transaction references (if applicable)
  • 📅 Dates and times
  • 📝 A timeline of what happened

Protect Yourself

Do not share passwords, banking PINs, one-time verification codes (OTPs), recovery phrases, or other highly sensitive credentials with FraudWatch or anyone else claiming to help.

Official Reporting

FraudWatch encourages users to report suspected fraud to the appropriate official organisations in their country. We provide educational guidance and help users organise information before making an official report.
