🚨 Latest Scam Alerts

💰 Investment Scams

Be cautious of guaranteed investment returns, fake trading platforms, and scammers pretending to be financial experts.

❤️ Romance Scams

Never send money to someone you've only met online, even if they appear genuine or claim to be in an emergency.

₿ Cryptocurrency Scams

Always verify crypto platforms, wallet addresses, and investment opportunities before transferring digital assets.

🛒 Shopping Scams

Be careful of fake online stores, unbelievable discounts, and sellers requesting payment outside trusted platforms.

📧 Phishing Emails

Never click suspicious links or open unexpected attachments. Always verify the sender before responding.

🤖 AI & Deepfake Scams

Scammers increasingly use AI-generated voices, images, and videos to impersonate trusted people. Always verify independently.

Our Mission

FraudWatch exists to educate, protect, and support individuals and businesses through fraud awareness, verification tools, scam reporting, recovery guidance, and practical educational resources.

We believe that knowledge, awareness, and early verification are among the strongest tools for reducing the impact of fraud.
