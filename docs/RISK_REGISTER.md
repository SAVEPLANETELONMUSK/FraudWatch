# FraudWatch Risk Register

## Risk

False or misleading reports.

Mitigation:
Moderator review before publication.

---

## Risk

Users assume "no match" means "safe."

Mitigation:
Display clear disclaimers.

---

## Risk

Outdated guidance.

Mitigation:
Regular content reviews.

---

## Risk

Privacy concerns.

Mitigation:
Collect only necessary information and explain how it is used.
