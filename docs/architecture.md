# FraudWatch Architecture

## Core Systems

### Verification Engine
Purpose:
Help users check suspicious information before acting.

Future Features:
- Website verification
- Email verification
- Phone verification
- Username verification
- Wallet verification

---

### Reporting Engine

Purpose:
Collect scam reports for review.

Future Features:
- Anonymous reporting
- Evidence uploads
- Case tracking
- Moderator review

---

### Recovery Engine

Purpose:
Guide victims after fraud.

Future Features:
- Recovery checklist
- Evidence organizer
- Recovery dashboard
- Country-specific guidance

---

### Education Engine

Purpose:
Teach people how scams work.

Future Features:
- Scam guides
- Videos
- Articles
- Safety quizzes

---

### Support Engine

Purpose:
Support victims emotionally and practically.

Future Features:
- Community resources
- Recovery stories
- Professional help directory
- Safety planning
