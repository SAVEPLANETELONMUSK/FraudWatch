⚠ Important Safety Reminder

FraudWatch provides educational guidance to help you recognise common fraud risks and make informed decisions.

Verification results should never be treated as proof that a person, company, website, email address, or organisation is legitimate or fraudulent. Always carry out your own independent verification before sending money, sharing personal information, or making important decisions.

For your protection, never share passwords, banking PINs, one-time verification codes (OTPs), recovery phrases, private keys, or other highly sensitive credentials with FraudWatch or anyone claiming to assist you.
