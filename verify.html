What FraudWatch Can Help With

📧 Email Verification

FraudWatch can identify that an input is an email address, explain common phishing warning signs, discuss the email provider or domain where appropriate, and recommend practical checks. An email address alone cannot reliably reveal the sender's physical location.

🌐 Website & Domain Review

Learn what to look for when assessing a website, such as suspicious domains, unrealistic claims, poor contact information, or other warning signs. Always verify important information independently before making payments.

📞 Phone Number Guidance

FraudWatch helps you recognise common warning signs associated with unexpected phone calls, impersonation attempts, and requests for urgent payments. Country codes can sometimes indicate where a number is registered, but they do not confirm who is calling.

🌍 IP Address Analysis

If you enter an IP address, FraudWatch can help explain what an IP address is and, in future versions, display general network information such as the apparent country or region using trusted lookup services. IP information should never be treated as proof of a person's identity or exact location.

🏢 Company Verification

Before sending money or sharing sensitive information, verify that a company is genuine by checking its official website, contact details, registration information where available, and other trusted sources.

💬 Username Verification

FraudWatch helps explain common impersonation techniques involving Telegram, WhatsApp, social media, and messaging platforms. Always confirm that you are communicating with the genuine account through independent official channels.
