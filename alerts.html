Why Scam Alerts Matter

🛡 Prevention

Learning about common scam tactics before encountering them can help reduce the risk of financial loss and identity theft.

📚 Education

Understanding how scams operate makes it easier to recognise warning signs and respond safely.

🌍 Community Awareness

Sharing accurate fraud awareness information helps families, friends, businesses, and communities stay informed about common scam techniques and emerging threats.

⚠ Stay Alert

Scammers frequently change their tactics. Always verify identities, websites, payment requests, and unexpected messages before taking action.

How to Protect Yourself

  • ✅ Verify identities independently.
  • ✅ Be cautious of urgent payment requests.
  • ✅ Never share passwords or one-time verification codes (OTPs).
  • ✅ Confirm website addresses before logging in.
  • ✅ Question offers that seem too good to be true.
  • ✅ Keep your devices and software updated.
  • ✅ Report suspected scams to the appropriate authorities.

Need to Report Something?

If you've encountered a suspicious website, phone number, email, social media account, or investment opportunity, you can contact FraudWatch for educational guidance or submit a report through the Report Centre.
