🚩 Common Warning Signs

  • Pressure to act immediately.
  • Promises of guaranteed profits.
  • Requests for cryptocurrency or gift cards.
  • Unexpected messages asking for money.
  • Requests to keep payments secret.
  • Poor grammar or suspicious email addresses.
  • Offers that seem too good to be true.

🛡 How to Stay Safe

  • Verify identities before sending money.
  • Research companies independently.
  • Use strong, unique passwords.
  • Enable two-factor authentication.
  • Keep devices and apps updated.
  • Never share one-time verification codes.
  • Question urgent requests for payment.

🔎 Verify Before You Trust

If you receive a suspicious message, investment opportunity, website, phone call, or email, use the FraudWatch Verify Centre before taking action.

🚨 What To Do If You've Been Scammed

If you believe you've been targeted by fraud, act quickly. Secure your accounts, preserve evidence, contact your bank or payment provider, and review our Recovery Centre for practical guidance.

📖 Fraud Prevention Checklist

  • ✔ Verify before paying.
  • ✔ Double-check websites and email addresses.
  • ✔ Never rush financial decisions.
  • ✔ Talk to someone you trust if unsure.
  • ✔ Report suspicious activity.
  • ✔ Continue learning about new scam tactics.
