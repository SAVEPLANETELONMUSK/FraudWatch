📋 Evidence Checklist

Before submitting a report, try to collect as much accurate information as possible.

  • 📷 Screenshots of conversations
  • 📧 Email addresses involved
  • 📞 Phone numbers
  • 🌐 Website links (URLs)
  • 💬 Telegram, WhatsApp or social media usernames
  • 💳 Transaction references (if applicable)
  • 📅 Dates and times
  • 📝 A clear timeline of events

🛡 Stay Safe

For your protection, never include passwords, banking PINs, one-time verification codes (OTPs), recovery phrases, private keys, or other highly sensitive credentials in your report.

If money has already been sent, contact your bank or payment provider as soon as possible and consider reporting the incident to the appropriate authorities in your country.

Need Help With Your Report?

If you're unsure what information to include or would like guidance before submitting a report, you can contact the FraudWatch Support Centre.

✅ Before You Submit

Please review your report before submitting it. Providing clear, accurate, and factual information helps improve fraud awareness and educational resources.

FraudWatch encourages responsible reporting. Do not knowingly submit false, misleading, or defamatory information. If you believe a crime has been committed, report it to the appropriate law enforcement or official reporting agency in your country.

Submitting a report to FraudWatch does not create an official police report. FraudWatch uses reports to improve fraud awareness, educational content, and future platform development.
