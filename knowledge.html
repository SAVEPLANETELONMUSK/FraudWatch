Fraud Prevention Checklist

  • ✅ Verify identities using trusted, independent sources.
  • ✅ Be cautious of urgent payment requests or pressure tactics.
  • ✅ Never share passwords, banking PINs, or one-time verification codes (OTPs).
  • ✅ Check website addresses carefully before signing in or making payments.
  • ✅ Question offers that seem unusually profitable or too good to be true.
  • ✅ Keep your devices, apps, and security software up to date.
  • ✅ Report suspected scams to the appropriate authorities in your country.
