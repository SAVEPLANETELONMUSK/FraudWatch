Recovery Centre

If you believe you've been scammed, acting quickly can help reduce further harm. Follow these steps and seek help from trusted organizations.

Recovery Checklist

1. Stop Further Payments

Do not send additional money, even if someone promises to recover your funds.

2. Contact Your Bank

Notify your bank or payment provider immediately to discuss available options.

3. Preserve Evidence

Save emails, messages, receipts, screenshots, wallet addresses, and transaction details.

4. Secure Your Accounts

Change passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and review account activity.

5. Report the Scam

Report the incident to the appropriate authorities in your country and help warn others.

6. Seek Support

Scams can have emotional as well as financial impacts. Speak with trusted people and access support resources.

You Are Not Alone

Many intelligent, careful people are deceived by sophisticated scams every year. Seeking help is a positive step toward recovery.
