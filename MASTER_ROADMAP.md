# FRAUDWATCH MASTER ROADMAP

## 🧭 Mission

FraudWatch exists to help people:

- Prevent fraud before it happens
- Verify suspicious activity safely
- Report scams responsibly
- Recover after fraud with guidance
- Access education and awareness resources
- Receive emotional and practical support

FraudWatch does NOT guarantee fund recovery.

FraudWatch does NOT declare guilt or fraud as fact.

FraudWatch provides **educational guidance and risk indicators only**.

---

## 🌍 Vision

To become a global digital safety platform that helps people recognize scams, reduce financial harm, and make safer online decisions.

---

## 🏗 Core Product Pillars

### 1. Prevent
Education, alerts, awareness content.

### 2. Verify
Check suspicious information before trust or payment.

### 3. Recover
Step-by-step guidance after suspected fraud.

### 4. Support
Emotional and practical help resources.

- Input type detection

## 📌 Development Rule

Every feature must:

- Support at least one core pillar (Prevent / Verify / Recover / Support)
- Respect user safety and privacy
- Avoid making absolute claims
- Be verifiable or clearly labeled as guidance

---

## 🧠 Product Philosophy

FraudWatch is not a detective system.

It is a **decision support system** that helps users:

- Think more clearly
- Verify more safely
- Recover more confidently
- Learn continuously

## Platform Values

- Trust through transparency
- Privacy by design
- Education before reaction
- Evidence before publication
- Compassion for victims
