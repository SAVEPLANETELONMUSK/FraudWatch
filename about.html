About FraudWatch

Building a safer digital world through education, awareness, and community support.

Who We Are

FraudWatch is an independent educational initiative dedicated to helping individuals and businesses recognise scams, strengthen digital awareness, and make informed decisions online.

Our Mission

Our mission is to educate, protect, and support people by providing practical fraud awareness resources, verification tools, and guidance that encourages safer online behaviour.

Our Vision

We envision a future where people everywhere have access to reliable fraud education, practical safety resources, and a supportive community that promotes trust, awareness, and resilience against scams.

Our Values

  • Integrity
  • Education
  • Transparency
  • Respect
  • Innovation
  • Community

Work With FraudWatch

We welcome opportunities to collaborate with educators, cybersecurity professionals, consumer protection organisations, financial institutions, technology companies, researchers, and community groups that share our commitment to fraud awareness and prevention.
