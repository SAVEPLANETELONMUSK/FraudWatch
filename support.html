Support Centre

Being scammed can affect your confidence, finances, and wellbeing. You are not alone, and support is available.

How We Support You

🤝 You're Not Alone

Many people experience fraud every year. Reaching out for help is a positive step.

🧠 Emotional Recovery

Take time to process what happened, talk to someone you trust, and seek professional support if you need it.

🛡️ Stay Safe

Learn how to recognize common scams and strengthen your digital security.

📚 Learn

Visit our education centre to understand how scams work and how to reduce future risks.

📋 Recovery Plan

Follow the Recovery Centre checklist to secure your accounts and preserve important evidence.

🚨 Report Fraud

Reporting scams can help authorities and warn other people about emerging threats.

Recovery Takes Time

It's normal to experience a range of emotions after fraud. If you're struggling emotionally or feel overwhelmed, consider speaking with a trusted person or a qualified mental health professional in your area.
